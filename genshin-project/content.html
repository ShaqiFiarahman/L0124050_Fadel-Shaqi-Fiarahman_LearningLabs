Elements in Genshin Impact

Tip: Each element has unique interactions and uses.

Genshin Impact features seven elements: Anemo, Geo, Pyro, Hydro, Cryo, Electro, and Dendro. Each element has its unique powers and interactions with other elements.

Elemental Reactions

In Genshin Impact, combining elements can trigger powerful reactions that deal extra damage or provide useful effects. Below are some key elemental combinations and their effects:

  • Pyro + Hydro = Vaporize - Increases damage based on the order of elements.
  • Cryo + Electro = Superconduct - Reduces physical resistance of enemies.
  • Electro + Anemo = Overloaded - Creates an explosion dealing massive damage.
Elemental Reaction Chart

This diagram shows how different elements interact with each other in Genshin Impact:

Anemo

Anemo is the power of wind, used by characters to control gusts and storms. It is highly effective in crowd control and spreading elemental effects.

Geo

Geo is the power of earth, used to create barriers and control stone. It excels in defensive strategies and creating shields.

Pyro

Pyro is the power of fire, capable of dealing intense damage through flames. It is effective in causing burning damage and triggering reactions.

Hydro

Hydro is the power of water, used for healing and fluid combat strategies. It also excels in triggering freezing and vaporizing reactions.

Cryo

Cryo is the power of ice, used to freeze enemies and control the cold. It is effective in crowd control and slowing down enemies.

Electro

Electro is the power of electricity, sparking powerful attacks and combos. It interacts with other elements to trigger powerful elemental reactions.

Dendro

Dendro is the power of nature, associated with plant-based abilities and reactions. It's still in development for certain characters.
